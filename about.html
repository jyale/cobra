About

Crypto-Book was created by John Maheswaran in collaboration with Danny Jackowitz, Ennan Zhai, David Isaac Wolinsky, Bryan Ford as part of the Dissent project in the computer science department at Yale University.

Full source code is available online for the encrypted Facebook message sender and for Crypto-Book Black Box.

If you have any questions/comments, get in touch!

We published the following paper on Crypto-Book in HotNets '13:

Crypto-Book: An Architecture for Privacy Preserving Online Identities
